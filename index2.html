Concept

Changelog is one of the most promising startups launching in early 2021. That’s a smart habit tracker with wide social networking functionality, designed the way it can be easily integrated with 3rd party services. It should be a great achievement hub for you and your apps, matching all the spheres you might need: fitness, gaming, self-education and many others. Create challenges, invite your friends and track each others’ success story together.

Like imagine you sign in and make a habit: «Swedish every day» lasting 2 years, you add some useful links into description and invite your friends, or even make that challenge public — so that you have a great leaderboard with a nice statistics on the completion. That’s just the basics of our product, learn more about it and its’ monetization in our presentation.
